How to use our services:

  1. Decide what you want to be built imagining the colours and styles that will be used - look on Instagram and Pinterest to explore plans to develope a visual imagination of the project
  2. Identify and measure the space that is going to be worked on - use this webpage to determine the size of the room you have as a rough estimate for its size
  3. Complete the survey and compare your budget to our qoute we had produce for your plans - take a look at materials you would like to use and cheaper alternatives to fit in your budget
  4. Once you have agreed to the qoute, we will begin the consultation process - consider the time period you want the job to be done in alongside plans that you have
  5. After the consultation process, we will begin the construction process
  6. During construction, additional services may be available to further enhance the project - freely contact us to communicate additional features to be included in our work
  7. Once the construction process is complete, we will have a final inspection to ensure that the work is up to standard
